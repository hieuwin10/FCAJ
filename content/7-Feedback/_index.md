> Below are my personal thoughts and feedback after the 12-week journey with the First Cloud Journey (FCJ) program.

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Learning & Working Environment**  
The learning environment at FCJ is extremely open and dynamic. The AWS community is very enthusiastic, and members are always ready to answer questions and help each other with hands-on Labs. This positive atmosphere gave me a lot of motivation to complete the program.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
I would like to express my special thanks to the Mentors and Team Admins. You not only provided detailed materials and dedicated guidance but also shared valuable real-world experience with AWS. The open-ended coaching method helped me develop my independent thinking and troubleshooting skills.

**3. Relevance of the Program to Personal Goals**  
The program strictly follows practical knowledge and perfectly aligns with my goal of pursuing Cloud Computing. The knowledge of Compute, Storage, Database, and Networking goes beyond theory and is directly applied to a real-world project.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Throughout the 12 weeks, I have learned a lot of new skills, from operating on the AWS Console and using the AWS CLI to designing and deploying a practical system. Besides, my teamwork, technical documentation writing, and reporting skills have also significantly improved.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
The internship team members all share a high spirit of support and learning. When implementing the final project, the team coordinated very smoothly, debugging together and finding solutions to difficult problems, creating an amazing teamwork experience.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?
  **Answer:** The massive, highly practical system of Labs, along with the dedication of the Mentor team.
- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?
  **Answer:** Sometimes, a few Labs feature an older version of the AWS interface compared to the current one. Updating them more frequently would make it easier for beginners.
- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?
  **Answer:** Absolutely. This is a perfect stepping stone for anyone who wants to enter the world of cloud computing with AWS.

### Suggestions & Expectations
- Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?
  **Answer:** Organize more live-coding practice sessions or solve real-world business case studies.
- Would you like to continue this program in the future?
  **Answer:** I sincerely hope to have the opportunity to participate in more advanced training programs or community activities organized by AWS Vietnam in the future.
