### Week 8 Objectives:

* Understand and apply data analytics and streaming services (Kinesis, Glue, Athena, QuickSight).

* Build an end-to-end Data Pipeline with Glue DataBrew and EMR.
* Design and visualize data through interactive Dashboards on QuickSight.
